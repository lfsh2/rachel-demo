Is This Right for You?

This Work Is
For You If…

I want to be honest with you. This work is specific, and it's not for everyone. Here's how to know if it's the right fit.

You're ready if…
  • You're exhausted from performing and genuinely want something different
  • You're willing to go slow and do the actual inner work
  • You believe (or want to believe) that God is actually for you
  • You're ready to be honest — with yourself and with a coach
  • You're a Christian woman who wants faith to feel real, not just correct
Honest Clarity

This Isn't
For You If…

I'd rather be upfront than waste your time or mine. Here's who this isn't the right fit for right now.

This may not be the right fit if…
  • You're looking for a quick fix or a 3-step formula
  • You need clinical mental health treatment (I'll always refer you to the right support)
  • You want someone to validate staying exactly where you are
  • You're not willing to examine your beliefs about God and yourself
  • You want advice-giving, not transformation-walking

Not sure? Book a free call and we'll figure it out together. I'll always be honest about whether this is the right fit — and point you somewhere better if it isn't.
